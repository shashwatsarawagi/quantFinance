Forward-start call option: Buying an option at t=0 for a strike price set at T1 later.
Shout call option: Allowed to reset strike price to asset if becomes anything higher
Parisian: payoff resets when in-barrier and accumulates for time outside
Parasian: same as parisian without resets
Correlation swap: exploit inefficiencies in pricing same instruments like index option vs basket of stock options

Balloon: Quantity increases when certain conditions met
Break forward: FX forward that can be terminated at certain times
Contingent premium option: A premium is payed on conditions
Coupe option: Strike is reset to min of underlying and previous strike
Extendible option/swap: expiration can be extended
Hawai'ian option: Asian and American haha
Himalayan option: Multi asset where best stock is removed at sampling times till one at expiration
HYPER option: High Yielding Performance Enhancing Reversible - American but multiexecution where each one flips C/P
